  • Tokyo
The world’s most populous metropolitan area, Tokyo is home to the Japanese Gov’t and the Imperial Family. The 23 Special Wards of Tokyo accommodate over 9 million people, while the entire prefecture has a staggering 13.3 million. There is everything from shopping, sports, and culture to fine dining and commerce. Whether you want to see a sumo tournament or eat the world’s best sushi, Tokyo has it all!
